03 — About me

A bit of
context

+20 years designing digital products. I started in editorial and advertising, went through creative direction, and found my place in product design.

I've worked with teams at Volkswagen Group, BBVA, Orange, Openbank, Andbank, Viajes El Corte Inglés and Atresmedia. Always in environments where design needs to scale and stay consistent.

My current obsession: reducing friction between design and development. Design Systems that actually work, tokens that sync, documentation that doesn't become obsolete the next day.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably sailing, tinkering with AI, or mentoring junior designers.
